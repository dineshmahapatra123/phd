# Qualitative Document Analysis

Seed note for Qualitative Document Analysis.

## Expanded Definition (Nayak, 2020)
A research method employed by Pradeep Nayak (2020) involving the critical examination of primary sources (gazetteers, government reports, laws, court judgments) and secondary literature to trace the historical evolution and political economy of land tenure systems.
