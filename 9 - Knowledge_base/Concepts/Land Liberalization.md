# Land Liberalization

A process of deregulating state control over land regimes, often advocated since the 1990s in India. It involves lifting restrictions on agricultural land transfers, promoting large-scale corporate farming, and encouraging foreign direct investment (FDI). It represents a rightward shift away from the traditional land reform agenda.
