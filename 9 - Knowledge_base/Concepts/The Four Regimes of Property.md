# The Four Regimes of Property

A standard classification framework: 1) Open Access (unregulated, prone to tragedy of the commons), 2) State Property (managed by government agencies), 3) Private Property (exclusively owned by individuals, enforced by state), 4) Commons Property (managed by a defined community with rules of inclusion/exclusion).
