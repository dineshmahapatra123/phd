# Neoliberal Land Policy

The contemporary policy shift in India from a redistributive justice model (e.g., land ceilings, tenancy reform) to a market-efficiency model. This policy paradigm prioritizes deregulation, ease of land acquisition for corporate capital, and formalization of property rights to create active land markets and stimulate economic growth.
