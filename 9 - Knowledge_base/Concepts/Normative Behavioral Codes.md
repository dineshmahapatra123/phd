## Standard Definition
The cultural values, customs, and informal norms that legitimize institutional arrangements and constrain human behavior. These codes often evolve very slowly.

## Scholarly Debate
Feder and Feeny (1991) point out that in developing countries, a lack of congruence between formal [[Institutional Arrangements]] (e.g., alienability of land) and normative codes (e.g., land as a communal/clan heritage) can lead to enforcement failures.

## PhD Application
Critical for understanding the "Record-Reality Gap" in Indian land records, where informal community norms often override formal legal titles.

## Measurement / Methods
Ethnographic studies, cultural analysis, and surveys on social legitimacy of laws.
