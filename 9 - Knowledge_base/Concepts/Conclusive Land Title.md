# Conclusive Land Title

Seed note for Conclusive Land Title extracted from Pradeep Nayak's book.

## Expanded Definition (Nayak, 2020)
A legal regime where the state guarantees the accuracy of the land title. Once a title is registered, it cannot be defeated by prior claims (indefeasibility). This eliminates the need for expensive historical title searches and significantly reduces transaction costs.
