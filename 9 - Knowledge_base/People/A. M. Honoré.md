# A. M. Honoré

Seed note for A. M. Honoré.

## Expanded Definition (Nayak, 2020)
A legal scholar who, in the 1960s, gave substance to the 'bundle of rights' concept by identifying eleven standard incidents of ownership (e.g., right to possess, use, manage, income, capital, security, transmissibility) common across mature legal systems.
