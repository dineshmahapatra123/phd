# David Feeny

**David Feeny** is an economist (formerly at McMaster University) specializing in the evolution of institutions and property rights, with a focus on agricultural development in Southeast Asia.

## Scholarly Lens: New Institutional Economics
Feeny's work focuses on the historical and evolutionary nature of institutional change. He emphasizes how changes in relative factor prices (land vs. labor scarcity) and the agricultural terms of trade drive the demand for more precise property rights in land.
