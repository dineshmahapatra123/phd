# Satish Y. Deodhar

## Scholarly Lens
**Satish Y. Deodhar** is a Professor of Economics at IIM Ahmedabad, specializing in identifying **Ancient Indian Antecedents to Economic Thought** (author of *Economic Sutra*). His lens connects modern institutional economics with ancient Indian philosophy and governance treatises (e.g., Arthashastra, Upanishads). He emphasizes the "Trustee" model of sovereignty versus the absolutist Western models.

## Core Bibliography
- **Deodhar, S. Y. (2019)**: *Economic Sutra: Ancient Indian Antecedents to Economic Thought*.
- **Ghosh, R. K., & Deodhar, S. Y. (2025)**: *"In the name of eminent domain": A historical and colonial perspective to land governance and land struggles in India*.

## Inter-Person Links

## Methodological Bias
Focuses on **Comparative Institutional Philosophy**. He maps ancient Sanskrit terms like **[[Vasundhara]]** and **[[Samudaya]]** to contemporary economic concepts of wealth and common property.
