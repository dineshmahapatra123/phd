## Scholarly Lens
P.S. Appu was a former IAS officer and Land Reforms Commissioner for the Government of India. His perspective is deeply rooted in administrative realities and policy implementation. He argues that the failure of Indian land reforms is not merely due to administrative or legislative loopholes, but primarily due to a profound lack of "political will".
