# Person: Edella Schlager

## Scholarly Lens
**Public Policy / Institutional Analysis**. Known for her collaboration with Elinor Ostrom in developing the conceptual and empirical foundations for comparing property-rights regimes.

## Core Bibliography
- "Property-Rights Regimes and Natural Resources: A Conceptual Analysis" (1992, with Ostrom)
- "Fishers' Institutional Responses to Common-Pool Resource Dilemmas" (1994)

## Inter-Person Links
- **Collaborator**: **[[Elinor Ostrom]]** (together developed the 5-right bundle framework).

## Methodological Bias
Focuses on **Common-pool resource** dilemmas. Uses cross-case comparative methods to identify how rules affect performance in fisheries and water systems.
