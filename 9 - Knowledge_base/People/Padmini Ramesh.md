# Padmini Ramesh

## Scholarly Lens
Padmini Ramesh is associated with the Kudumbashree National Resource Organization (NRO). Her research focuses on land governance, agrarian relations, and the implementation of social welfare schemes in India, specifically in the context of Telangana.
