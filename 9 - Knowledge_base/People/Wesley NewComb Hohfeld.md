# Wesley NewComb Hohfeld

Seed note for Wesley NewComb Hohfeld.

## Expanded Definition (Nayak, 2020)
A legal theorist (1913) who revolutionized the understanding of property by deconstructing it into a set of fundamental legal relations (rights, privileges, powers, immunities) between people, moving away from Blackstone's absolutist conception.
