# Method

A research design, analytical approach, or methodological framework used in the literature or considered for the thesis. Created and updated by `/compile-phd`.

## Required Frontmatter

```yaml
---
type: Method
Paper_Linked: "[[Paper where method is used]]"
Last_Processed: "YYYY-MM-DD"
Status: Seed | Sapling | Evergreen
---
```

## Required Body Sections

1. **Description** — what the method or design is
2. **Strengths / Weaknesses** — when it works and when it fails
3. **PhD Relevance** — how it may inform the thesis

## Rules

- Check `9 - Knowledge_base/index.md` before creating — no duplicates
- Backlink to the source paper's master note
- Keep methods distinct from concepts: methods explain how research is done; concepts explain what is being theorised
