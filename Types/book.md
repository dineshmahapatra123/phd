# Book

A monograph, edited volume, or major book-length source note.

## Required Frontmatter

```yaml
---
type: Book
Last_Processed: "YYYY-MM-DD"
Status: Seed | Sapling | Evergreen
APA_Citation: ""
Authors:
Year:
Publisher:
ISBN:
---
```

## Required Body Sections

1. **Citation** — full bibliographic details
2. **Central Argument** — the book's main thesis
3. **Chapter Map** — relevant chapters and their claims
4. **Key Passages** — curated quotations with page numbers
5. **Concepts / Methods** — ideas or methods to shatter into the knowledge base
6. **PhD Relevance** — how the book informs the thesis
7. **Links** — related Concepts, Topics, People, Methods, Papers, and Chapters

## Rules

- Use for book-length sources, not journal articles
- Preserve chapter-level nuance; do not compress the book into a single summary
- Link important concepts to the knowledge base when they mature
