# Clip

A clipped article, webpage, essay, newsletter, or read-it-later item.

## Required Frontmatter

```yaml
---
type: Clip
Last_Processed: "YYYY-MM-DD"
Status: Inbox | Processed | Archived
Source:
Author:
URL:
Date_Published:
Tags:
---
```

## Required Body Sections

1. **Source** — publication, author, date, and URL
2. **Captured Text / Summary** — the clipped material or concise summary
3. **Key Claims** — the claims worth retaining
4. **Use in PhD** — whether it supports background, context, method, or writing
5. **Next Action** — archive, convert to Query, cite, or shatter into the knowledge base

## Rules

- Preserve the original URL when available
- Distinguish journalistic/contextual evidence from peer-reviewed sources
- If a clip becomes part of formal PhD prose, verify and format citation through `TISS_STYLE.md`
