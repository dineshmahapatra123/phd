# Codex Workflow: arxiv-impact

Purpose: identify impactful recent papers for a field/year and prepare them for ingestion.

## Steps

1. Formulate a broad scholarly query.
2. Search arXiv and related scholarly sources.
3. Triage for relevance to institutions, property rights, credible commitment, land governance, or the user's specified topic.
4. Verify impact signals such as citations, venue, author prominence, code/social uptake when relevant.
5. Return a top list with contribution, impact rationale, and PDF/landing-page links.
6. Suggest `ingest-paper` for selected items.
