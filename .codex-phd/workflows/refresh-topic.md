# Codex Workflow: refresh-topic

Purpose: update a living topic synthesis from the current knowledge base.

## Steps

1. Locate the requested note in `9 - Knowledge_base/Topics/`.
2. Read the topic note, `index.md`, and relevant notes from `Concepts/`, `People/`, `Methods/`, and `sources/`.
3. Identify new evidence or contradictions since the topic was last processed.
4. Append or update the synthesis while preserving older useful evidence.
5. Use `### ⚠️ Contradiction` for conflicting findings.
6. Update `Last_Processed` to the current date.
7. Summarize what changed.
