Kalia Yojana is a flagship scheme of the Odisha government aimed at providing financial assistance and livelihood support to farmers, sharecroppers, landless agricultural laborers, and vulnerable households in the state.

Launched in 2018, the scheme has gained widespread popularity and has been hailed as a model for other states to follow.

## Extension of Kalia Yojana 2024

Ahead of the upcoming Lok Sabha and assembly elections, the Odisha government has announced the extension of its flagship farmers’ welfare scheme, the Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation (KALIA) Yojana. This decision comes as a significant boost for the agricultural community in the state, with a total outlay of Rs 6,029.70 crore sanctioned for the purpose.

The state cabinet, led by Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik, approved three proposals related to the extension of the KALIA scheme, underscoring the government’s commitment to the welfare of farmers and their families. Since its inception in 2018-19, KALIA has played a crucial role in supporting the livelihoods of a large segment of Odisha’s population, which is predominantly dependent on agriculture.

One of the key components of the extended scheme is the provision of financial assistance to eligible landless agricultural households. These households, which have already received the full instalments under the scheme, will now receive an additional financial aid of Rs 2,000. This initiative aims to further alleviate financial burdens and enhance the well-being of vulnerable farming communities.

Under the KALIA Yojana, beneficiary farmers have been receiving Rs 4,000 per year to support their agricultural activities. Additionally, the government has introduced the KALIA scholarship to ensure the educational development of children from beneficiary families. By facilitating access to professional and technical education, this scholarship program aims to empower youth and foster human capital development in rural areas.

To streamline the implementation of the scheme and ensure timely disbursement of benefits, the cabinet has approved modified release dates for KALIA assistance.

### Objectives of Kalia Yojana:

The main objective of Kalia Yojana is to support the agricultural and rural economy of Odisha by providing financial assistance to farmers, sharecroppers, and landless agricultural laborers. The scheme aims to enhance the livelihoods of these groups and ensure their financial stability by providing direct benefit transfers. The scheme is also aimed at improving the productivity and sustainability of agriculture and allied activities in the state.

### Features of Kalia Yojana:

Assistance to Farmers: Under the Kalia Yojana, farmers receive financial assistance of Rs. 10,000 per annum in two installments of Rs. 5,000 each. This assistance is provided to small and marginal farmers who have less than 2 hectares of cultivable land.

Assistance to Landless Agricultural Laborers: The scheme also provides financial assistance of Rs. 10,000 per annum to landless agricultural laborers, who work in the fields of other farmers. This assistance is provided to help them meet their household expenses and support their livelihoods.

Interest-Free Crop Loan: Under the scheme, farmers are also eligible for an interest-free crop loan of up to Rs. 50,000. This loan is provided to help farmers meet their crop-related expenses and is interest-free.

Support for Farm Mechanization: The scheme also provides support for farm mechanization by providing financial assistance to farmers for the purchase of small equipment such as pump sets, power tillers, and other implements.

#### Impact of Kalia Yojana:

Since its launch, Kalia Yojana has had a significant impact on the lives of beneficiaries. The scheme has provided financial assistance and livelihood support to farmers, sharecroppers, and landless agricultural laborers. It has helped them to improve their standard of living, reduce their dependence on moneylenders, and meet their household expenses. The scheme has also helped to increase agricultural productivity and sustainability in the state.

#### Check KALIA Yojana List Odisha 2024

- Open the official website

[https://kaliaportal.odisha.gov.in/](https://kaliaportal.odisha.gov.in/)

- Click the “Beneficiary list” option from menu bar from the home page

![Kalia Yojana](https://updateodisha.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Kalia-Yojana-1-1024x488.jpg)

Kalia Yojana

- A new page will appear on the screen and you need to select your district name
- Then select your Block/ ULB and then GP
- Click the “View” button and you may get the PDF link
- Then click on the PDF link and enter the captcha code
- Click “Submit” to open the PDF

Kalia Yojana list

- Now you have to check your Kalia ID, Village name, your name, father/ husband name and gender to confirm your name in the Kalia Yojana list
